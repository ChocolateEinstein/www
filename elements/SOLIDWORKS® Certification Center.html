Welcome to SOLIDWORKS Certification Center

SOLIDWORKS certifications can be used as a benchmark to measure your knowledge and competency with SOLIDWORKS software. Exams focus on the fundamental skills necessary to ensure your company’s overall success using SOLIDWORKS. For additional information, see the SOLIDWORKS Certification website.

Certification Exams are available 24x7 by downloading the Tangix TesterPRO Client software from the link to the right and install the software on your computer. For a list of available exams and information on pricing and availability, see the SOLIDWORKS Certification website.

System Requirements

To run a Certification Exam you need to download and install Tangix TesterPRO Client. Downloading and installing this software takes less than a minute and Administrator privileges are normally not required.

Supported operating systems are Windows XP SP3 and later. Both 32- and 64-bit versions are supported.

Internet access is required when starting and finishing the Exam.

Most proxies and firewall solutions are supported.

Technical Information

If you experience problems with the certification system or Tangix TesterPRO Client, please consult the information on the VirtualTester Support web before contacting support.

This website is hosted by a Swedish company and as a company operating in the European Community (EC) we are required to provide vistors to this website information about cookies and how personal data collected through our website.
Personal data includes all types of information which directly or indirectly can be associated with a living natural person. Examples of personal data include names, postal addresses and e-mail addresses. The Personal Data Act (1998:204) (PuL) contains provisions on how personal data may be processed.

Controller of personal data

As a service provider Tangix Design & Development AB (registered in Danderyd, Sweden) do collect and store personal data on behalf of the Licensee of the service. It is the Licensee that is the controller of personal data for this website. The name of the Licensee is listed in the footer of the website.
As a service provider Tangix Design & Development AB will never share, sell or otherwise reveal personal data to thrid part unless required to disclose pursuant to a valid order or judgement issued by a court, arbitrators or any other governmental body having jurisdiction over the service.

IP numbers

Tangix Design & Development AB saves visitors’ IP numbers. These are used for visit statistics for the website and are also saved for security reasons. Collected IP numbers are treated as personal data.

Cookies on the website

This website contains what are referred to as "cookies". A cookie is a small text file that the website you are visiting asks to save on your computer.
One "session" cookie and three "ordinary" cookies are used on this website.

The website's cookies and their use

Session cookies disappear when you shut down your web browser and are thus not saved, while ordinary cookies are saved on your computer for longer.

Session cookies
One session cookie is used to keep a track of a user's activities while navigating this website and to provide security.

Ordinary cookies
Two ordinary cookies are used by the website to record changes to the visual appearance of the website (such as column widths and selected language).
One ordinary cookie is used by the website if the "Remember Me" options is checked at login. A unique identifier is then created which is matched with a record in the website's database at next visit to the website.
No personal data is stored in any of the cookies used by the website.

How to avoid cookies
If you do not wish to accept cookies, your web browser can be set to automatically deny the storage of cookies or to inform you every time a website requests permission to store a cookie. Previously stored cookies can also be deleted via the web browser. Visit the web browser’s help pages for more information.
